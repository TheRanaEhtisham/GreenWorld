As widely known for their warm welcomes and for the luxe minimalism of their interiors, the 19 floor hotel hosts 130 rooms, an internationally acclaimed Signature restaurant and an organic herb garden becomes part of the restaurants farm-to-fork concept. While high on the skyline of Mercury Tower stands a reimagining of their London icon, the Radio Bar, all curved forms, soft lights and sensational views.
SPA AND WELLNESS

In the buildings limestone-lined vaults, a jewel of a spa

Underground, youll find an extraordinary limestone spa set in a post-WWII telecommunications hub, immaculately refurbished. Designed as a space to relax and recharge, the spa adds a perfectly luxurious destination for wellbeing. Residents and visitors can enjoy a wide array of exquisite on-site luxury amenities, including a state-of-the-art gym and outdoor running track with incredible views at roof level and five swimming pools with both indoor and outdoor options to choose from.
RETAIL

GYM For All Wants And Needs. A carefully curated shopping experience

Most shopping areas evolve over time, Mercury Towers has taken the chance to curate a perfect balance of stores to meet every need and desire. A world-renowned luxury department store is the centerpiece, flanked by food shops, global design brands and independent fashion and lifestyle stores, together offering an entirely unique collection of places to shop.
FOOD & DRINK

Creation of a food and drink destination. From artisan delights to internationally renowned franchises

Mercury Towers welcomes a brand new open-air and indoor food and dining destination to Malta. This unique food hub introduces a curated selection of the very best international concept restaurants, bars and cafs; all situated around the vibrant square of Mercury Towers. Anchoring the development, the main restaurant will be a global draw, from a chef of international renown, specially created for Mercury Towers. The different dining concepts and wide selection of international cuisines reflect Mercury Towers diverse culinary culture and respond to a growing appetite for delicious and delectable food.
RADIO BAR

33-floors above ground, a breathtaking Radio Bar. Rooftop panoramas and, beyond, the Mediterranean Sea

Welcome to Maltas newest addition to the bar scene. Set atop Mercury Tower, the Radio Bar is all curved forms, soft lights and sensational views of the St Julians skyline. Lounge beside the illuminated pool and order from one of the best-stocked bars in the region, while the mixologists work their magic and DJs spin ambient soundtracks to memorable evenings. The 33rd floor will be the place to be seen and to see, with an adrenaline-charging glass viewing platform open to visitors, allowing a thrilling perspective over the island from above.

ENTERTAINMENT ARENA

An action-packed Cinema

Mercury Towers epitomize luxury-living in an exciting and vibrant urban landscape. Whether you seek a new experience or some family fun, there is something for everyone at Mercury Towers Entertainment Arena, an underground (and soundproof) amusement park by 360 Karting. The arena includes an indoor electric Go-Karting track, offering beginners and experienced drivers the ultimate driving experience, arcade games, axe throwing and an indoor ice-skating rink, a cool escape for when temperatures rise outdoors.
